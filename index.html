What Can They Do?

Hellen Keller: (1880-1968) Deaf-blind author, activist, and lecturer who earned a BA and became a professional speaker. She wins the Presidential Medal of Freedom and was elected to the Women's Hall of Fame.
Marlee Matlin: (1965-present) Two-time Oscar-winning actress and deaf rights advocate who is completely deaf in one ear and has about 20% hearing in the other.

Education

Gallaudet University is the only university in the world that is specifically for deaf and hard of hearing students. It was founded by Thomas Gallaudet in 1864 and located in Washington, D.C.. It gathers the best deaf students from all over the world.
